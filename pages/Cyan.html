Cyan

Cyan is a fairly new kingdom, founded after the war. Its purpose is to get rid of the monsters that leave the Forbidden Forest. It was only later, that they found out about Alias and started the fight with him and his subordinates. Along the border of the Forbidden Forest, they tied a red thread to the trees so that everyone knew where the danger zone started.

There were occasionally subjugation teams that entered the forest. They mainly consisted of the royal knights of Cyan. Usually, the king or the queen led them, but as time went by, they aged and reliable commanders took care of it instead of them. The prince and the princess of Cyan are coming of age soon, and after that, it will be their turn to lead the troops.

The royal heirs both are very capable. Her Highness the Royal Princess learned healing magic from Lady Ethernia, the Fairy of Cyan. She's also a reliable commander and a great fighter. Although, in combat, her brother, His Highness the Royal Prince is the real prodigy. He could be considered a genius of their generation, not many can fight with a sword as well as he can. The inheritance of the throne between them is still undecided, but both are willing to step down if the other is named successor.

The town is mostly peaceful, the Royal Family considers the well-being of their people top priority. The Royal Highnesses have a habit of hanging around in the town, so they are very close with the residents. Most of their people live by growing fruits, wheat, and other plants. There are also bakers, craftsmen, and a few others. The monsters' meat is edible, so they don't have to breed livestock.

The Kingdom's other speciality is Gilla. It's a type of carrot, which looks a bit like a huge, deformed potato. By huge, think of it as a size of two fists. When you eat it, Gilla tastes sweet, then the aftertaste is magical. Sadly, it only lives in harsh environments, so only Cyan is able to produce them, which is the main source of their income beside the monsters.
