Chapter 4

Ehm... Rude much? Frowned the girl.

Riat had crossed the river. On the bridge. The bridge, that was a few minutes of walk away. The bridge, that his dear friend decided to ignore. Likely because he deemed it 'too far'. That is, if he can believe anything the girl says. The girl, who is, by the way, really annoying. And rude. Who is she to call HIM rude?! And what's with that overy casual attitude?? She could be lying about everything. Yeah. She likely is.

Heyyyy.... heeeey? Old maaaaan~

Robin called out in a bored voice noticing Ryat getting lost in thought.

What?! And I'm not old, I'm just a bit older than Prince!

Yeah, yeah... talking about Prince though, I think we should take him back to Ethernia's place to warm him up

Ryat almost choked. Almost.

How do you know about that place?! It's supposed to be classified-!

Huuuh? But you know about it too, no? Yet you guys are just a thief and his grumpy friend~

Thief-?! Wha-

So? Are you coming or not?

Ryat's eyes widened as the girl easily lifted up his friend.

Who are you, really...?

A secret makes a woman, woman she winked.

A what? Ryat looled at her taken aback.

I know, I know, wrong planet~

Ryat frowned. His head is starting to hurt from all the bullsh- right. Breathe in, breathe out. There. Anger management 101.

But I'll be carrying him

Suit yourself~

Ryat took his friend from the weird girl and they headed to the wooden house.
