Divia

Divia is among the oldest countries of the content. Its history goes all the way back till the Age of Dragons. The people of Divia, including the Royal Family, are very proud of their past. They have multiple architectural structures that remained from the ancient times. Many buildings are covered in gold, the decoration of the streets is majestic.

The Royal Palace is not an exception either. Divia is not called the Kingdom of Roses just for show. The garden of the Royal Palace is filled with all kinds of roses from all around the continent. Not just the palace, the streets of Divia are also blooming.

This magnificent Kingdom has two princesses. The older is Her Highness the Crown Princess Rosalia and her younger sister is Her Highness Lilith. Taught by their parents, both have a very proud nature. Her Highness Rosalia embodies perfection. She is smart, beautiful, and kind. Her peers can't help but adore her. In contrast, Her Highness Lilith still acts immature. People say that she's jealous of her sister and that she will never be as good as Her Highness Rosalia.

Divia is a flourishing Kingdom. People are proud to be born in Divia and they all love the Crown Princess. About Her Highness Lilith there are all sorts of rumors going around, but overall, citizens still think that she is cute. As the Kingdom is rich, people don't have to face many hardships in order to survive. They often make cosmetics, beauty products and jewelry.

Divia's specialty is perfume. They mainly work with all kinds of roses, but that doesn't mean they don't use other scents as well. Their perfumes are well-known in the whole continent. The King and Queen of Divia own one of the most successful perfumeries, which goes by the name of Ramor. His Highness is a rather powerful businessman and he expects the same from the princesses.
