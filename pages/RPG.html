Transcription of RPG

Song playing in the background.

Song: Role-Playing Game by Soraru, Mafumafu, Urata and Sakata

Text: Song: Role-Playing Game

Text: Artist: Pace

Bigger text: RPG

As the text disappears, Robina pops up from below, her eyes are shining.

Robina: So everyone, it's teamwork OK? (いいんですか皆さん チームワークですよ)

Tiny Annabelle and Ryat pop up from below, behind Robina. Ryat looks bored, Annabelle enthusiastic.

Ryat and Anna: Understood! (了解)

Everybody disappears, Prince appears sitting on a red sofa with a controller in his hands. He looks annoyed.

Prince: Ugh, who's the one pulling the team from behind? (おいおい 足引っ張ってんの誰だよ)

Anna appears again, now in normal size. She shrugs.

Anna: It's Soraru-san (here: Prince) who's progressing too fast. (どんどん先に行っちゃうそらるさんでしょ)

Prince gets more annoyed.

Prince: I'm just opening the paths so everyone can play more easily. (俺はみんながやりやすいように 道きり開いてるだけ)

Anna is also annoyed.

Anna: That's just your excuse, right? To us, it's giving us troubles. (それはそっちの言い分でしょ こっちは迷惑してんだよ)

Anna brightly looks towards Ryat.

Anna: Right? Sakata (here: Ryat)? (なあ坂田)

Camera moves to Ryat who is also holding a controller and is panicking.

Ryat: Uwaaaaa!!! I'm gonna be killed by Mafumafuuuuu (here: Robina)!!! (うわあああ まふまふに殺されるうううううう)

Camera switches to Robin who now has white fox ears and a tail. She menancingly looks into the camera. The camera moves closer to her face.

Robin: Hyahahahaha! Everyone dieeee! (ひゃはははは! 全員死ねぇ!)

Camera shows Prince who is displeased, then pouts.

Prince: Aah!! Me too? Mafumafu (here: Robin), I definitely won't forgive you! (ああ!! 俺まで まふまふ お前だけは許さん!!)

Ryat is shown running away from Robin, terrified, screaming.

Ryat: Please someone help meeeeee (誰か助けてくださああああい)

Robin chases him, then jumps, turns into a little white fox and lands onto Prince's lap.

Anna pops up shouting.

Anna: Where has all the teamwork gone guys?! (もうチームワークどこ行った! お前らあああああ)

Ryat is startled, he stops running and looks at Anna who is in the middle. On the other side, Robin is sitting on Prince's lap, on the sofa. Prince is hugging her. They are all looking at Annabelle.

Song continues in the background.

Text: Thanks for watching!

Beside the text is Robin. As the volume of the song goes down, the screen is slowly fading into black.

Video ends.
