Robina

Robina is the unofficial guardian fairy of Cyan. Although she claims that she is only there to protect Prince, she cares deeply about the well-being of the people.

Appearance

Robina's body was originally granted by the planet. However, it doesn't affect her appearance nor how her body works.

Robina has Sorrell Brown hair and Dark Emerald Green eyes. In her younger years, her hair is shoulder length, but it grows a lot during her travels. After she returns to Cyan, she cuts her hair because it inconveniences her during a fight.

Robina wants to believe that her height is at least average, but she's rather short. Her body is really fit but still skinny. She claims that it's because of her genes.

She wears many different but comfortable outfits. Her most common ones are her Chara-cosplay from UNDERTALE, and a red dress that Prince gave her as a gift. The people of Cyan mostly have winter-coloured outfits, so she usually stands out a bit.

Personality

Robin is a self-confident person, who greatly values her freedom. Despite her often-cynical remarks, she has a kind heart and cares a lot about her friends. She highly values life, as she prefers not to kill anyone if it's not necessary. This became one of her reasons to get stronger. Strong enough, that no one has to die around her.

During her journey throughout the continent, Robin developed a considerable distrust towards high-ranking people. Seeing that many of them are not suitable for their position, she became more and more sceptical when meeting new ones. This was somewhat eased after she returned to Cyan and met Prince. She shows great appreciation towards the rulers who she finds capable.

Robin is not led by a sense of justice; she mainly does things for her own entertainment. Later, her motivation becomes protecting Prince and helping him achieve whatever he wants. Even then, she doesn't really feel hate towards her opponents as she's more interested in their reasons for acting the way they do.

Background

Robin was around 4 or 5 years old when she woke up on the planet for the first time. Ethernia found her, and realizing the child's situation, she took her in. As Ethernia looked after many children in Cyan while their parents were working, Robina found many friends early on.

For these years, she was taught magic by Ethernia. She had a carefree childhood, but studying magic is highly difficult, so she struggled a lot with it. Despite that, she was determined to master all kinds of magic as there wasn't any magic in her original world and after seeing Ethernia use it, it became her dream to be able to do the same.

Years later, she was finally good enough to be called a Fairy. Because Ethernia was already the Guardian Fairy of Cyan, Robina had to go on a journey, to find herself a country. During these years, she learned all kinds of fighting styles and techniques from all around the continent; her magic also became much stronger as she experienced many unexpected situations.

It was when she met Leo and they traveled together for a long time. Robina also became acquainted with many of the rulers of the regions, including Idette, Vicky and Serpa.

After being notified about Ethernia's death, she returned to Cyan and became Prince's personal guard .

Abilities

Robin's biggest strength is her magic. Her main element is air, but she is capable of using any kind of magic. Because she started at a young age and she's rather talented, there's hardly anyone who could overpower her.

Her other main element is space magic, which was Ethernia's speciality. More accurately, portals. She had a hard time mastering it, but now she can teleport not just to anywhere on the planet but to other dimensions as well. Her magic easily slips through the barriers of the 『Tales』. Although she can't modify the storylines, she has a habit of entering the worlds of different tales and interacting with the characters. Sometimes, she even brings Prince along.

With the power to enter different tales, she has the ability, to copy their powers and use them wherever she wants to. Of course, it would be dangerous if she overused it, but she insists that she has a poor memory so if she needs to defeat someone and there is suitable magic to deal with it, she might not realize that in the moment of the fight.

Even without magic, Robin is considerably strong. She can win against many of the knights in Cyan, who are considered the most powerful elites on the continent after Miriam's army . Her main weapon is a dagger, but she can fight with a sword as well. She uses her air magic to make her body lighter, so she can do acrobatic movements with ease. Of course, this is a tactic she doesn't use while sparring with the knights, but she often uses it while she trains with Prince as she is the only one who can match his strength but only this way.
