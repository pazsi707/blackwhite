Chapter 1

A place where you can find all the stories of the world. Each having its own planet or even dimension. Not knowing about each other, they co-exist peacefully. Only with the strongest space magic can one cross the well-guarded borders of these worlds. In the middle of it all, there's a tiny planet. This planet has a secret. Every now and then, a lost soul arrives, and the planet grants them a body. When they fall asleep, they return to their original worlds into their original bodies and think of it as a dream. But then again, when the night comes, they find themselves on the tiny planet in their new bodies, just like they've never left in the first place. On this planet there are a handful individuals who use magic. The people call them 『Fairies』.
